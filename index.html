Get to Know Alice the Great

I am a 4-year old Ridgeback/Boxer mix with a dash of Pitbull. I was named after Alice from the Resident Evil movies because my mom wanted me to be a badass.

As it turns out, I'm afraid of most things, including fireworks and strangers with hats - but I'm working on it.

Favorite Pastimes

I enjoy playing with my brother, Bishop, stealing all of the space in the bed, pretending I'm a lapdog, and chasing after any and every beam of light or reflection.

Favorite Tricks

I pride myself on being a pretty smart pup. Sure, I can do the basics (sit, lie down, stay, etc.) But I can also back up, crawl, give kisses, and go fetch each toy by name.

Let's be friends!
